BLOG_FOCAL!!!

Blog_Focal is a blogging platform where you can create, edit, organise your blog.


  • You can combine text, images, link, videos in your blogs.
  • You can comment and enrichen the knowledge of your peers.
  • You can report the inappropriate content by clicking on the flag.
  • You can save the post of other bloggers to make your own collection of knowledge.
  • You can upvote or downvote the post of other bloggers.
  • You can share your and others posts via link, file, or screenshots.

    • Come let us build social relations with readers and bloggers.
